Why I Made This

Honest answer: the assignment was worth 100 points. Here's the longer version.

The task sheet had a portfolio table. A poster was worth 25 points. A website was worth 100. I did the maths. A website also felt like the most impressive option that didn't involve making a rap, which was the other 100-point choice. No offence to anyone who made a rap.

I chose Wyndham because it's where I live. I wasn't going to research a place I'd never been to. Turns out there's actual data in Wyndham's own health reports about how youth programs are basically nonexistent here — so I didn't have to make anything up. The quote on the home page is a real quote from a real report. Genuinely convenient.

The rover being a van that comes to you was genuinely my idea. I thought: why do all health programs make you go somewhere? What if it just showed up instead? Turned out that's actually a reasonable design solution. I'm not saying it's Nobel Prize level but I'm not not saying that either.

This is a school assignment. Please don't actually try to contact WynHealth Rover. It doesn't exist yet. The website is very real. The van is not. If you are a Wyndham City Council employee who has found this: hello, please fund it.
